One simulation step
===================

0. State at the start of a step
--------------------------------
The grid holds, at every point, the ADM 3+1 variables:
- gamma_{ij}  — the spatial metric (how distances are measured on the 3D slice)
- K_{ij}      — the extrinsic curvature (how the slice is embedded in 4D spacetime;
                 encodes the "velocity" of the metric)
- alpha = 1, beta^i = 0 — lapse and shift (geodesic slicing: observers free-fall
                           with no spatial drift)


1. Evaluate the EM source (tornado_matter_grid)
------------------------------------------------
At every grid point, the rotating spotlight selects the currently active vortex source.
For that source:

1. 4-potential A_mu(x) — a static magnetic flux tube centred on the active source
   location, evaluated via finite differences in all 4 spacetime directions.
2. Faraday tensor F_{mu nu} = d_mu A_nu - d_nu A_mu — encodes the electromagnetic
   field strengths.
3. EM stress-energy tensor T_{mu nu} — the quadratic EM field energy/momentum/stress,
   computed from F_{mu nu} and the current 4-metric (assembled from gamma_{ij} plus
   geodesic-slicing g_{00} = -1).
4. ADM projection -> AdmMatter { rho, J^i, S_{ij} }:
   - rho  = T_{00}: EM energy density (seen by the normal observer)
   - S_{ij} = T_{ij}: spatial stress (radiation pressure)

This produces one AdmMatter per grid point. The source is held fixed across all four
RK4 stages — it is an externally prescribed driver.


2. Compute the right-hand side (geodesic_rhs_with_matter)
----------------------------------------------------------
For each interior point (the boundary 2-cell band is frozen), compute
d_t gamma_{ij} and d_t K_{ij}:

2a. Spatial geometry at the point
   - Read gamma_{ij} and K_{ij} from the grid.
   - Invert gamma_{ij} -> gamma^{ij} (3x3 Gauss-Jordan).
   - Centred finite differences on the neighbouring grid values -> d_k gamma_{ij}.
   - Christoffel symbols:
       Gamma^k_{ij} = 1/2 gamma^{kl} (d_i gamma_{jl} + d_j gamma_{il} - d_l gamma_{ij})

2b. Curvature
   - Centred FD of Christoffels at the +-1 neighbours -> d_nu Gamma^rho_{kappa mu}.
   - Riemann tensor:
       R^rho_{sigma mu nu} = d_mu Gamma^rho_{nu sigma} - d_nu Gamma^rho_{mu sigma}
                           + Gamma^rho_{mu lambda} Gamma^lambda_{nu sigma}
                           - Gamma^rho_{nu lambda} Gamma^lambda_{mu sigma}
   - Ricci tensor:
       3R_{ij} = R^k_{ikj}  (contraction)

2c. Vacuum ADM RHS (geodesic slicing)

   d_t gamma_{ij} = -2 K_{ij}

   d_t K_{ij}^vac = 3R_{ij} + K K_{ij} - 2 K_{ik} K^k_j

   where K = gamma^{ij} K_{ij} is the trace.

2d. Matter correction

   d_t K_{ij}^matter = -8pi S_{ij} + 4pi gamma_{ij} (S - rho)

   This is the term that couples the EM source to the geometry — it acts as a
   forcing term that pushes K_{ij} away from its vacuum trajectory.

   Total: d_t K_{ij} = vacuum part + matter correction.


3. Advance in time (RK4)
--------------------------
Standard 4th-order Runge-Kutta on the field vector (gamma_{ij}, K_{ij}) at all
interior points simultaneously:

   k1 = RHS(y_n)
   k2 = RHS(y_n + dt/2 * k1)
   k3 = RHS(y_n + dt/2 * k2)
   k4 = RHS(y_n + dt   * k3)

   y_{n+1} = y_n + dt/6 * (k1 + 2*k2 + 2*k3 + k4)

Steps k2-k4 recompute the full geometric RHS (Christoffel -> Riemann -> Ricci ->
ADM equations) at the intermediate field values, but reuse the same matter source
from step 1. Boundary values are frozen throughout.


4. Record diagnostics (every output_every steps)
-------------------------------------------------
- H_L2:           L2 norm of the Hamiltonian constraint
                  3R + K^2 - K_{ij} K^{ij} = 0
                  which should be zero for exact solutions — measures numerical accuracy.
- K_offdiag_rms:  RMS of the off-diagonal K components {K_{01}, K_{02}, K_{12}} —
                  zero in flat space, grows as the source seeds rotational extrinsic
                  curvature.
- gamma_perturb_rms: RMS deviation of gamma_{ij} from delta_{ij} — measures how far
                  the spatial metric has been deformed from flat.
- max_rho_EM:     Peak EM energy density across the grid.


The physical picture
---------------------
Each step, the active vortex source pushes K_{ij} via the matter correction. Over many
steps, as the spotlight rotates around the ring, it deposits extrinsic curvature at
successive azimuthal angles — the signature of angular momentum being injected into the
geometry. The spatial metric gamma_{ij} then responds to the accumulated K through the
d_t gamma = -2K equation, developing off-diagonal structure that reflects the growing
spacetime rotation.
